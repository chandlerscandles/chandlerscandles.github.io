Hey! 👋 I'm Chandler and I like to make candles from beeswax...

…which is quite fitting since the name Chandler is derived from candlemaker!

I make candles that are naturally unscented, simply beeswax and nothing more, or candles scented with lavender, cedar, or bergamot. I currently offer candles in two sizes, small (~200g) and large (~300g).

My candles are made from beeswax sourced from bees right here in the UK. For me, working with the highest quality ingredients is really important, and beeswax offers a huge range of benefits.

Beeswax is non-toxic and environmentally friendly, and beeswax candles have a longer burn time than their soy or paraffin counterparts. They have a delightful scent naturally, and when additional scent is added, it only enhances the experience. Most importantly, perhaps, is that beeswax has proven health benefits, acting to remove pollutants and allergens from the air as it burns. It’s the healthier option for candles, ensuring a relaxing and calming candle experience.

I use wooden wicks in my candles as well. I love the crackling sound they produce as they burn, and I know you will too!
